MakeMyTrip Hotel Listing Guide for Indian Hotels

By OnlineHotelier Insights Team Updated May 2026 7 min read

What is MakeMyTrip for Hotels?

India's #1 OTA — 60%+ Domestic Market Share

MakeMyTrip (MMT) is India's largest online travel agency. For most Indian hotels, it is the single biggest source of online bookings. Getting your listing right on MMT directly impacts your occupancy.

Step-by-Step: How to List Your Hotel on MakeMyTrip

1 Register on MMT Partner Central

Go to partners.makemytrip.com and click "Register as Hotel". Fill in your property name, city, and contact details. You will receive an OTP verification on your registered mobile number.

2 Complete KYC Verification

Upload the following documents:

  • GSTIN certificate
  • PAN card (hotel entity or proprietor)
  • Cancelled cheque or bank statement
  • Hotel registration / trade licence
  • Property photos (minimum 10, at least 1280×960px)

KYC approval takes 3–7 working days.

3 Set Up Your Property Listing

Once approved, complete your property profile: room types, amenities, policies (check-in time, pet policy, cancellation terms), and upload all photos. Incomplete listings rank lower in MMT search results.

4 Load Rates and Availability

Set your base rates for each room type and open inventory for the dates you want to sell. Use the MMT extranet or connect a channel manager to sync rates automatically.

5 Go Live and Monitor

Once content is complete and rates are loaded, your listing goes live. Monitor your ranking, review score, and conversion rate from the MMT Partner Central dashboard.

MMT Extranet: Managing Your Listing Day-to-Day

The MMT extranet (Partner Central) is where you manage everything after going live:

Function Where to Find It
Update rates and availability Inventory & Rates section
View and manage bookings Reservations tab
Update photos and content Property Details section
View reviews and respond Reviews & Ratings tab

Pro Tip: If you list on more than 2 OTAs, managing MMT manually becomes time-consuming. A channel manager syncs rates and inventory to MMT automatically — one update, all channels updated.

Photos and Content That Drive More Bookings

MMT's algorithm ranks listings partly on content quality. Hotels with better content get more impressions and higher conversion rates.

Photo Requirements

  • Minimum 10 photos; 25+ photos significantly improves conversion
  • Resolution: 1280×960px minimum (1920×1080px recommended)
  • Must include: room interior, bathroom, property exterior, common areas, dining area
  • No watermarks, no text overlays, no heavy filters

Content Checklist

  • Property description: 150+ words, mention key landmarks and distance from railway/airport
  • Amenities: Mark every available amenity accurately — misleading info leads to bad reviews
  • Room types: Create separate listings for each room category (Standard, Deluxe, Suite)
  • Policies: Set clear check-in/check-out time, cancellation policy, and extra bed charges

MakeMyTrip Commission, Payouts and TDS Deductions

Item Details
Base commission 12–23% depending on property type and city tier
Smart Deals / promotions Additional 5–8% discount funded by hotel (optional)
TDS deduction 1% on gross booking value (u/s 194-O)
GST on commission 18% GST charged on MMT's commission amount
Settlement cycle Weekly, 7–14 days after checkout
Payment mode NEFT/RTGS to registered bank account
Tax Note: MMT deducts TDS (1%) and GST on commission before remitting. Always reconcile your MMT settlement report against your accounts. See our TDS & TCS guide for OTA payments for the full breakdown.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my hotel on MakeMyTrip?

Register at partners.makemytrip.com with your GSTIN, PAN, bank details, and property documents. After KYC verification (3–7 days), set up your listing with photos and rates, then go live.

What commission does MakeMyTrip charge hotels?

MMT charges 12–23% commission depending on property type, city, and programs opted into. Budget hotels in tier-2/3 cities typically pay 18–20%. TDS (1%) and GST on commission are deducted before settlement.

How long does MakeMyTrip hotel listing approval take?

KYC approval takes 3–7 working days after all documents are submitted. Your listing goes live once content and rates are complete.

How does MakeMyTrip pay hotels?

MMT settles weekly, 7–14 days after guest checkout, via NEFT/RTGS to your registered bank account after deducting commission, TDS, and applicable charges.

What documents are needed to list on MakeMyTrip?

GSTIN certificate, PAN card, cancelled cheque, hotel registration or trade licence, and a minimum of 10 property photos at 1280×960px or higher.
