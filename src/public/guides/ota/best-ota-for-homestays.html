Vacation Rentals vs Homestays

While the OTA strategy is similar, these are distinct property types:

  • Vacation Rentals: Entire homes, villas, or apartments rented to guests. Owner may not be present. Common in Goa, Lonavala, Alibaug, Manali, Coorg
  • Homestays: Rooms within a host's home or attached property. Host interaction is part of the experience. Popular in Kerala, Himachal, Northeast India, Pondicherry

Both property types compete on experience, uniqueness, and personal touch — not on amenities like a hotel would.

1. Airbnb

Why It's #1

Airbnb essentially created the vacation rental and homestay category. It's where experience-seeking travelers go first when looking for non-hotel stays.

  • Commission: 3% host fee (guest pays a separate service fee of 14-16%)
  • Payment: Airbnb collects, pays out 24 hours after check-in
  • Strength: Global brand recognition for alternative stays, strong community, review trust
  • Best for: All vacation rentals and homestays, especially unique or experiential properties

Why Airbnb Works Differently

Unlike hotel OTAs, Airbnb charges a much lower host commission (3% vs 15-25%). The platform makes money from guest service fees instead. This makes it the most profitable OTA for hosts.

Key Insight: Airbnb's 3% host commission means you keep ₹4,850 on a ₹5,000 booking. On MakeMyTrip at 20% commission, you'd keep only ₹4,000. Airbnb is by far the most profitable channel for vacation rentals and homestays.

Airbnb Optimization Tips

  • Superhost status: Maintain 4.8+ rating, 90%+ response rate, and low cancellations
  • Instant Book: Enable it — Airbnb heavily favors properties with instant booking
  • Professional photos: Airbnb offers free professional photography in major cities
  • Personal welcome message: Customize your response to each booking — Airbnb guests value personal touch
  • Guidebook: Create a local guidebook with restaurant, transport, and activity recommendations

2. MakeMyTrip / Goibibo

Why It Matters

MakeMyTrip launched a dedicated "Homestays & Villas" section to compete with Airbnb. It brings India's largest domestic travel audience to your property.

  • Commission: 15-20%
  • Payment: Weekly settlement
  • Strength: Massive Indian user base, trust factor, holiday package integration
  • Best for: Domestic travelers who prefer booking through familiar platforms

When MakeMyTrip Beats Airbnb

  • Indian families who don't use Airbnb (older demographics, smaller cities)
  • Properties near pilgrimage sites or domestic tourist hotspots
  • Holiday weekends when Indian travelers plan quick getaways

3. Booking.com

Why It Works

Booking.com has expanded beyond hotels into apartments, homestays, and vacation homes. Their "Homes & Apartments" filter drives targeted traffic.

  • Commission: 15-18%
  • Payment: Pay at property or monthly invoice
  • Strength: International travelers, trusted brand, flexible cancellation policies
  • Best for: Properties in areas with international tourist traffic

Booking.com Tips for Homestays

  • Select "Homestay" or "Vacation Home" as your property type (not "Hotel")
  • Highlight what makes your space unique in the description
  • International guests often filter by "Free cancellation" — consider offering it for advance bookings

4. Agoda Homes

Why It's Worth Adding

Agoda Homes is Agoda's dedicated section for non-hotel stays. It brings Asian travelers — a market Airbnb doesn't dominate in India.

  • Commission: 15-20%
  • Payment: Monthly settlement
  • Strength: Asian traveler market, competitive in price-driven searches
  • Best for: Properties in Goa, Kerala, Rajasthan, and other destinations popular with Asian tourists

When to Prioritize Agoda Homes

  • Your area receives Southeast Asian, Japanese, or Korean travelers
  • You want to diversify beyond Airbnb + MakeMyTrip
  • Your listing isn't getting enough visibility on Booking.com

5. Cleartrip

Why It's Growing

Cleartrip's homestay section is newer but benefits from Flipkart's user base. It's an easy additional channel with growing domestic traffic.

  • Commission: 15-18%
  • Payment: Weekly settlement
  • Strength: Flipkart integration, domestic weekend getaway market
  • Best for: Properties within driving distance of major cities (weekend escape market)

Listing Setup Essentials

  1. Property description: Tell a story. What's the experience? Don't just list features
  2. Photos: Natural light, wide angles, show living spaces, kitchen, outdoor areas, views, and local surroundings
  3. House rules: Be clear about check-in times, smoking, pets, parties, and noise
  4. Amenities checklist: Wi-Fi, parking, kitchen, washing machine, AC, hot water — list everything
  5. Local area guide: Nearby restaurants, grocery stores, ATMs, and attractions
  6. Pricing: Set different rates for weekdays, weekends, and holidays

Managing Multiple Platforms

The biggest challenge with vacation rentals on multiple OTAs is avoiding double bookings. Solutions:

  • iCal sync: Most platforms support iCal calendar sync (free but can be slow to update)
  • Channel manager: Tools like Hostaway, Guesty, or Lodgify sync in real-time across all platforms
  • Airbnb as primary: Some hosts use Airbnb as their main calendar and block dates manually on other platforms

Legal Requirements in India

Homestays and vacation rentals have specific regulations:

  • Registration: Many states require homestay registration (Kerala, Himachal, Goa have specific policies)
  • GST: Required if annual revenue exceeds ₹20 lakhs (₹10 lakhs in special category states). See our GST guide
  • Guest registration: Form C required for foreign guests within 24 hours. See our GRC & Form C guide
  • Fire safety: NOC may be required depending on state and property size
  • Local permissions: Check municipal and society/community rules about commercial use of residential property
