Best OTAs for Resorts & Leisure Properties in India

By Hotel Playbook Team 7 min read

Top 5 OTAs for Resorts & Leisure Properties

1. MakeMyTrip • 2. Booking.com • 3. Agoda • 4. Cleartrip • 5. International Pick

Leisure properties — beach resorts, hill station retreats, wellness spas, and heritage properties — thrive on these platforms. Your 5th pick depends on which international travelers visit your destination.

What Makes Leisure Properties Different

Resorts and leisure properties have unique distribution challenges compared to city hotels:

  • Seasonal demand: Sharp peaks (holidays, festivals, winter/summer) and deep off-seasons
  • Longer stays: Average 2-4 nights vs 1-2 for city hotels
  • Package-driven: Guests want meal plans, activities, and experiences bundled
  • Advance bookings: Travelers plan leisure trips weeks or months ahead
  • Visual decision-making: Photos and reviews matter more than location convenience

1. MakeMyTrip / Goibibo

Why It's #1 for Leisure

Indian families plan holidays on MakeMyTrip. Period. Their holiday packages section, "MyBiz" for group trips, and deep domestic reach make it essential.

  • Commission: 15-25%
  • Strength: Holiday packages, domestic leisure travelers, festival season traffic
  • Best features for resorts: Meal plan integration, package deals, "Long Weekend Getaways" promotions

Resort-Specific Tips

  • List MAP and AP plans — leisure travelers prefer all-inclusive packages
  • Participate in seasonal campaigns ("Monsoon Deals", "Summer Escapes")
  • Use MLOS (Minimum Length of Stay) during peak weekends to maximize revenue

2. Booking.com

Why It Matters for Resorts

Booking.com attracts international leisure travelers who discover Indian destinations. Their "Resorts" category filter sends targeted traffic to your listing.

  • Commission: 15-20%
  • Strength: International travelers, "pay at property" model, strong review ecosystem
  • Best for: Goa, Kerala, Rajasthan, Himachal — destinations with international appeal

Resort-Specific Tips

  • Enable "Free cancellation" for bookings made 7+ days in advance — international travelers need this flexibility
  • Highlight spa, pool, and activity amenities prominently
  • Use their "Property Page Boost" during your low season to fill rooms
Key Insight: Resorts in Goa, Kerala, and Rajasthan get 30-50% of their bookings from international travelers. Booking.com is usually the #1 source for these international guests. If you're in these states, Booking.com may be more important than MakeMyTrip for your property.

3. Agoda

Why It Works for Leisure

Agoda's strength in Asia-Pacific means it sends significant traffic to Indian resort destinations, especially from Southeast Asian, Japanese, and Korean travelers.

  • Commission: 15-22%
  • Strength: Asian traveler market, aggressive discounting model
  • Best for: Beach resorts (Goa, Andaman), wellness retreats (Kerala, Rishikesh)

When Agoda Shines

  • Your destination has direct flights from Bangkok, Singapore, or Kuala Lumpur
  • You're in a wellness or yoga retreat (hugely popular with Asian travelers)
  • Your competition isn't actively using Agoda (opportunity to rank higher)

4. Cleartrip

Why It's Growing for Leisure

Cleartrip's "Activities" and "Holiday Packages" sections bring a different type of domestic traveler — often younger, tech-savvy, looking for experiences.

  • Commission: 15-20%
  • Strength: Flipkart integration (SuperCoins), growing holiday packages
  • Best for: Weekend getaways, experience-driven leisure travelers

Resort-Specific Tips

  • List experience packages (adventure, spa, cultural) alongside room-only rates
  • Weekend deals perform well — Cleartrip users plan short getaways
  • Flipkart sale events (Big Billion Days) drive hotel booking spikes

Seasonal Pricing Strategy on OTAs

Resorts need dynamic pricing more than any other property type:

Season OTA Strategy Pricing Approach
Peak (Oct-Mar, festivals) Reduce discounts, set MLOS 2-3 nights Rack rate or slight premium
Shoulder (Apr-May, Sep) Run targeted promotions, early bird offers 10-20% below peak
Off-season (Jun-Aug monsoon) Heavy OTA promotions, flash sales, mobile-only deals 30-50% below peak
Pro Tip: During off-season, consider listing exclusive OTA-only packages that include meals, spa credits, or local experiences. A ₹5,000 room with ₹2,000 worth of inclusions at ₹6,000 total feels like better value than a ₹6,000 room-only rate, even though your cost is similar.

Photo Strategy for Resorts

Leisure travelers make emotional decisions. Your photos need to sell the experience:

  1. Hero shot: Pool/beach/mountain view at golden hour — this is your listing thumbnail
  2. Room with a view: Show the balcony/window view, not just the bed
  3. Dining experience: Plated food at your restaurant with ambiance visible
  4. Activities: Guests enjoying the spa, pool, adventure activities
  5. Aerial/wide shots: Show the full property and surroundings
  6. Local flavor: Nearby attractions, cultural elements, nature

Aim for 30-40 photos minimum. Properties with 30+ photos get 25% more bookings on average.
