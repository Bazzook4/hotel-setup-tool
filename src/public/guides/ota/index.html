What Are OTAs?

Online Travel Agencies (OTAs) are platforms that allow travelers to search, compare, and book hotels online. They're a crucial distribution channel for Indian hotels, typically generating 40-70% of bookings for most properties.

Major OTAs in India:

  • MakeMyTrip & Goibibo - Dominant domestic players (same parent company)
  • Booking.com - Global leader, strong for international guests
  • Agoda - Popular in Southeast Asia
  • Expedia - Global reach, corporate bookings
  • Yatra - Indian OTA with corporate focus
