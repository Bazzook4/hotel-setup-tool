OTA: Online Travel Agency Explained

By Hotel Playbook Team 6 min read

What is OTA?

OTA = Online Travel Agency

Websites and apps that allow travelers to search, compare, and book hotels online. Examples: MakeMyTrip, Booking.com, Goibibo, Agoda, Expedia.

How OTAs Work

  1. Hotel lists rooms: You upload room types, rates, availability to the OTA
  2. Guest searches: Traveler searches for hotels in your city
  3. Guest books: Guest selects your hotel and completes payment
  4. You get booking: Reservation details sent to your hotel
  5. Guest stays: Guest checks in, enjoys their stay
  6. OTA takes commission: 15-25% of booking value deducted

OTA Commission Breakdown

Example: Guest books room for ₹5,000 on MakeMyTrip (20% commission)
  • OTA Commission: ₹1,000
  • GST on Commission (18%): ₹180
  • TDS (1%): ₹50
  • Your Payout: ~₹3,770

OTA Pros & Cons for Hotels

Advantages Disadvantages
Access to millions of travelers 15-25% commission on each booking
No upfront marketing cost Rate parity requirements
Reviews build credibility Limited guest data access
Instant payment processing Dependency on OTA algorithms
Global reach Guest loyalty goes to OTA, not you

OTA vs Direct Bookings

Factor OTA Booking Direct Booking
Commission 15-25% 0% (or 3% for payment gateway)
Guest Data Limited Full access
Marketing Cost Included You pay for ads, SEO
Reach Global Limited to your marketing
Loyalty To OTA To your hotel
Pro Tip: Aim for 60% direct + 40% OTA mix. Use OTAs for visibility, but incentivize direct bookings with best rate guarantees, free upgrades, or loyalty points.

OTA Optimization Tips

  1. Quality Photos: Invest in professional photography. It's the #1 factor in click-through rate.
  2. Complete Profile: Fill every field - amenities, policies, nearby attractions.
  3. Respond to Reviews: Reply to ALL reviews within 24 hours, especially negative ones.
  4. Competitive Pricing: Use rate shopping tools to stay competitive.
  5. Availability: Keep inventory open on all channels. Close only when truly sold out.
  6. Mobile Rates: Offer mobile-only discounts for app bookings.
  7. Genius/VIP Programs: Participate in OTA loyalty programs for better visibility.

OTA Extranet

Each OTA provides an "extranet" - a dashboard where you manage:

  • Room rates and availability
  • Promotions and discounts
  • Booking confirmations
  • Guest messages
  • Reviews and ratings
  • Performance analytics
  • Payment and invoices
Time Saver: Use a Channel Manager to update rates/availability across all OTAs from one place instead of logging into each extranet separately.
