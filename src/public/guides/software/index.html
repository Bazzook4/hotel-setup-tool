Popular Hotel Software in India

Here are some commonly used hotel software solutions in the Indian market:

All-in-One Solutions

  • eZee Absolute - Popular cloud PMS with channel manager, booking engine, POS
  • Hotelogix - Cloud-based PMS for small to mid-size hotels
  • Djubo - All-in-one solution for independent hotels
  • IDS Next (FortuneNEXT) - Enterprise PMS for larger properties
  • Stayflexi - Modern cloud PMS for budget hotels

Channel Managers

  • RateGain - Channel manager and revenue intelligence
  • AxisRooms - Channel manager focused on Indian market
  • SiteMinder - Global channel manager with Indian support
  • eZee Centrix - Channel manager from eZee

Accounting & Finance

  • Tally Prime - Most popular accounting software in India
  • Busy Accounting - GST-compliant accounting
  • Zoho Books - Cloud accounting with hotel integrations

POS Systems

  • eZee BurrP! - Restaurant POS from eZee
  • POSist - Cloud POS for restaurants
  • Torqus - F&B management and POS
