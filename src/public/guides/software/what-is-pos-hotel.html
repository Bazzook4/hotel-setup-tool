What is POS in Hotels? Point of Sale System

By OnlineHotelier Insights Team 5 min read

What is a Hotel POS?

POS = Point of Sale System

Software for billing at restaurants, bars, coffee shops, and other hotel outlets. Integrates with PMS for room charging and centralized reporting.

What Does Hotel POS Do?

Function Description
Order taking Capture food & beverage orders
Billing Generate GST-compliant bills
Room charge posting Post F&B charges to guest room account
KOT generation Kitchen Order Tickets for kitchen display
Table management Track table status, reservations
Inventory deduction Auto-reduce stock as items sold
Reports Sales, item-wise, waiter-wise reports

POS-PMS Integration

The most critical feature for hotel POS is integration with your Property Management System:

  • Room charge posting: Guest orders coffee, charge goes to room 305 instantly
  • Guest verification: Verify guest name and room before posting
  • Credit limit check: Alert if guest exceeds credit limit
  • Consolidated billing: All F&B charges appear on final room bill
  • Night audit: F&B revenue included in daily reports
Without Integration: Staff manually enters room charges in PMS = errors, delays, missed charges. With integration = instant, accurate, automatic.

Key POS Features for Hotels

  • Multiple outlets: Manage restaurant, bar, pool cafe from one system
  • Touch screen interface: Fast order entry for servers
  • Split bills: Split by item, by guest, equal split
  • Modifiers: "No onion", "Extra spicy", special requests
  • Happy hour pricing: Time-based promotional rates
  • Combo/packages: Meal deals, buffet packages
  • Multi-payment: Cash + card + room charge on same bill
  • GST compliance: Proper tax calculation, GSTIN on bills

POS Hardware Requirements

  • Touch screen terminal: Main billing station
  • Tablet/iPad: Tableside ordering
  • Kitchen display: Digital KOT screen
  • Receipt printer: Thermal printer for bills
  • KOT printer: Kitchen ticket printer
  • Cash drawer: For cash transactions
  • Card machine: EDC/POS terminal for cards
Pro Tip: Go for cloud POS. Access reports from anywhere, no data loss if terminal fails, automatic updates.

Choosing the Right POS

  1. PMS compatibility: Must integrate with your hotel PMS
  2. Ease of use: Staff should learn in 1-2 hours
  3. GST compliance: Proper tax handling for India
  4. Offline mode: Works if internet goes down
  5. Support: Local support in your language
  6. Scalability: Add outlets easily
  7. Inventory link: Integration with stores management
