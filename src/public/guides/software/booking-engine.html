Booking Engine: Get More Direct Bookings

By OnlineHotelier Insights Team 6 min read

What is a Booking Engine?

Your Hotel's Own Online Booking System

A booking engine (IBE - Internet Booking Engine) lets guests book rooms directly on your website. Zero commission, full guest data, complete control.

Why You Need a Booking Engine

The Math: 100 room nights/month at ₹3,000 ADR via OTA (20% commission) = ₹60,000 lost to OTAs. Same bookings direct = ₹60,000 saved (minus ~3% payment gateway).
OTA Booking Direct Booking
15-25% commission ~3% payment gateway only
Limited guest data Full contact info, preferences
OTA owns the relationship You own the guest relationship
Rate parity constraints Offer exclusive direct rates
Delayed payments Instant payment to your account

How Booking Engine Works

  1. Guest visits your website
  2. Enters dates and guests: Search widget on your site
  3. Sees room options: Available rooms with rates and photos
  4. Selects room and extras: Add-ons like breakfast, airport transfer
  5. Enters details: Guest information form
  6. Pays securely: Credit card, UPI, net banking
  7. Gets confirmation: Instant booking confirmation email
  8. You get the booking: Directly in your PMS

Key Features to Look For

  • Mobile responsive: 60%+ bookings happen on mobile
  • Multiple payment options: Cards, UPI, net banking, wallets
  • PMS integration: Bookings flow directly to your system
  • Multi-language: English, Hindi, regional languages
  • Promo codes: Create discount codes for marketing
  • Add-ons/Packages: Upsell breakfast, spa, transfers
  • Rate comparison widget: Show your price vs OTAs
  • Google Hotel Ads: Connect to meta-search

Driving Traffic to Your Booking Engine

Having a booking engine isn't enough. You need to drive traffic to it:

  • Google Hotel Ads: Show rates on Google search results
  • Social media: "Book Direct" links on Instagram, Facebook
  • Email marketing: Past guests, inquiries
  • Best rate guarantee: Promise lowest rate on your site
  • Exclusive perks: Free breakfast, late checkout for direct bookers
  • Retargeting ads: Target website visitors who didn't book
  • WhatsApp: Share booking link with inquiries
Pro Tip: Add a "Book Direct & Save" banner on your OTA listings directing guests to your website. Many OTAs allow this in listing descriptions.

Booking Engine vs OTA

Factor Booking Engine OTA
Commission 0% (+ payment gateway ~3%) 15-25%
Guest data Full access Limited
Traffic source You generate it OTA provides it
Setup effort Requires marketing List and wait
Control Complete OTA's rules

Ideal Direct Booking Mix

Target: Aim for 30-40% direct bookings. Use OTAs for visibility and new guest acquisition, convert repeat guests to direct bookers.

Setting Up Your Booking Engine

  1. Choose provider: Based on PMS compatibility and budget
  2. Setup payment gateway: Razorpay, CCAvenue, PayU
  3. Add room types: Photos, descriptions, amenities
  4. Set rates: Base rates, seasonal rates, packages
  5. Configure policies: Cancellation, deposit requirements
  6. Embed on website: Widget or full page integration
  7. Test thoroughly: Make test bookings on all devices
  8. Launch & promote: Drive traffic to your direct booking page
