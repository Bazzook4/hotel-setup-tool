Frequently Asked Questions

What is OTA commission and how does it work?
OTA (Online Travel Agency) commission is a percentage fee charged by platforms like MakeMyTrip, Booking.com, and Goibibo for each booking they generate. Typically ranging from 15-25%, this commission is deducted from your room rate before settlement. The OTA collects payment from the guest and remits the balance to you after deducting their commission, GST on commission, and applicable TDS/TCS.
Why is there GST on commission (18%)?
OTA services are classified as intermediary services under GST law, attracting 18% GST. This GST is charged on the commission amount, not on your room rate. What is TDS and TCS on hotel bookings?
TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) at 0.1%: Under Section 194-O, e-commerce operators must deduct TDS on gross amount of sales. This is adjustable against your income tax liability.

Is the commission calculated on base rate or total amount?
OTA commission is typically calculated on the base room rate (before GST). The guest pays room rate + GST, but commission is charged only on the room rate portion. This calculator follows the standard industry practice where commission is applied to the pre-tax amount.
