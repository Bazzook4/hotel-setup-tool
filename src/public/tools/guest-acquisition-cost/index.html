Frequently Asked Questions

What is the standard OTA commission in India?

Standard commission ranges from 15% to 20% depending on the platform. MakeMyTrip and Goibibo typically charge 15-18%, Booking.com 15-20%. This is on top of any promotions or boosters you opt into.

What is a Visibility Booster and does it pay off?

A Visibility Booster (Booking.com), Preferred Partner or similar programme increases your listing rank in exchange for 5% or more additional commission. It only pays off if the extra bookings it generates exceed the additional cost. Use this calculator to see the net revenue impact before activating one.

Is direct booking always more profitable?

Usually yes, but not always. If you are spending heavily on Google Ads and offering a large direct discount, your effective cost can approach OTA levels. The goal is to keep direct acquisition cost below 8-10% of room rate, which is still well below a typical OTA commission.

What payment gateway charges should I expect in India?

Razorpay, PayU and CCAvenue typically charge 2-3% per transaction for credit/debit card payments. UPI transactions are generally lower, sometimes 0-1%. Use 2% as a safe average for planning purposes.
