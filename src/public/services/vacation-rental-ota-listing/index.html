OTA Platforms We List You On

Airbnb
Best for Homestays & Unique Stays

Global reach with strong India demand. Best for independent homes, unique properties, and experiences. Guests search by vibe — not just location.

Host fee: 3% (split) or ~15% (host-only)
Booking.com
Best for Apartments & Villas

World's largest accommodation platform. Strong for urban apartments, holiday villas and properties near tourist destinations. Instant Book preferred.

Commission: 15–18%
MakeMyTrip
Best for Indian Domestic Travellers

India's #1 travel platform. Essential for reaching domestic travellers during long weekends, holidays and peak seasons. Also covers Goibibo.

Commission: 12–18%

Strong for Leisure & Beach

Popular with South-East Asian and international travellers. Strong in Goa, Kerala, hill stations and beach destinations.

How It Works

  1. Chat with Us on WhatsApp

    Tell us about your property — type, location, number of rooms, and when you want to go live. Takes 5 minutes. We ask the right questions.

  2. Share Your Property Details & Photos

    Send us photos, amenities list, and any documents needed (GST, property address, ID proof for OTA verification). We create a checklist for you.

  3. We Build & Optimise Your Listing

    We write your title, description, set up room types, configure pricing and policies, and upload everything across the OTAs you choose.

  4. Your Listing Goes Live

    Typically within 3–5 business days. We send you the live listing links and walk you through how to manage incoming bookings.

  5. Ongoing Management (Optional)

    Need ongoing help? We can manage your rates, calendar, guest messages and listing optimisation on a monthly retainer so you can stay hands-off.
