What is the Business hotel website theme and who is it for?

The Business theme is a website design built for city hotels, corporate properties, airport hotels and budget business hotels across India. It leads with a structured, no-nonsense layout: sticky Book Now button always visible, KPI stats bar showing rooms, location and facilities, and separate sections for corporate bulk enquiries and meeting room bookings.

Pricing starts at ₹15,000 (excluding GST) — the most affordable theme — for a 6-page site with WhatsApp inquiry. The Standard package at ₹25,000 adds a booking engine synced with MakeMyTrip, Goibibo, Booking.com and Agoda. Ideal for Bengaluru tech park hotels, Mumbai business hotels, Pune corporate properties and Delhi airport hotels. The site is live in 7 days.

Fast Loading

No heavy imagery. Grid-based layout loads in under 2 seconds on 4G. Business travellers booking on the move do not wait for slow websites.

Location Section

Dedicated section with distances from key landmarks — airport, metro, tech parks, hospitals. Embedded map. Business guests make location decisions first.

Mobile-First

Clean mobile layout. The sticky Book Now button, room rates, and amenity list all display clearly on any Android or iPhone screen without zooming or horizontal scrolling.
