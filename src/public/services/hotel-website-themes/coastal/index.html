Quick Answer
What is the Coastal resort website theme and who is it for?

The Coastal theme is a website design built for beach resorts, backwater properties, and lakeside hotels in India. It leads with a booking availability widget above the fold — the first thing a guest sees is the calendar to check their dates. The light airy blue-to-sky palette, wave motifs, and large photography placeholders are designed to sell the feeling of a coastal holiday, not just a list of room types.

Pricing starts at ₹25,000 (excluding GST) for a 6-page site with WhatsApp inquiry. The Standard package at ₹35,000 adds a full booking engine synced with MakeMyTrip, Goibibo, Booking.com and Agoda. The site goes live in 7 days. Ideal for Goa beach resorts, Kerala backwater stays, Andaman properties, and lakeside retreats in Nainital and Udaipur.

What the Coastal Theme Includes

Every element is designed to move a browsing guest to a booking decision as fast as possible.

📅

Booking Widget Above the Fold

The availability checker appears in the first viewport. Guests set their dates without scrolling. This is the highest-converting placement for leisure properties.

📸

Lifestyle Photography Layout

Full-width image sections with minimal text overlay. The photography does the selling. Caption text is secondary — atmosphere is primary.

🌊

Wave & Horizon Motifs

CSS-drawn wave separators between sections and a sun-horizon element in the hero. No raster images — loads fast on Indian mobile networks.

🤿

Activities Section

Tile layout for water sports, snorkelling, sunset cruises, kayaking, yoga and guided treks. Activities drive room nights — this section earns its place.

🏖️

Package & Offer Tiles

Seasonal packages with room-night pricing, inclusions, and direct inquiry CTA. Monsoon specials, honeymoon packages and festival offers all display cleanly.

🛏️

Villas & Rooms Section

Room types displayed with large photos, key features, occupancy, rate and a check-availability button. Supports beach huts, garden villas and sea-view suites side by side.

📱

Mobile-First Design

Every layout adapts to Android and iPhone. The booking widget is the first element on mobile — no scrolling required to start a reservation.

📷

Instagram Feed Integration

Optional integration of your Instagram grid into the footer area. Guest-generated content from your property is the most trusted social proof for leisure bookings.

Direct booking advantage: Resort guests that book through MakeMyTrip or Booking.com generate 15–18% commission on every reservation. A guest who books via your website costs nothing. A ₹10,000/night resort at 70% occupancy over 30 rooms pays roughly ₹1.1 crore a year in OTA commission at 15%. Even converting 30% to direct cuts that by ₹33 lakh. Your website pays for itself in its first week live.
