Quick Answer
What is the Heritage hotel website theme and who is it for?

The Heritage theme is a website design built for Indian boutique properties where the building itself is the attraction — Rajasthan havelis, Kerala heritage homes, hill station cottages, old Goa villas, Himachal heritage properties. It uses warm terracotta-to-ivory tones, Georgia serif typography, and ornamental detailing that signals history and character without a single word.

Pricing starts at ₹25,000 (excluding GST) for a 6-page site with WhatsApp inquiry. The Standard package at ₹35,000 adds a booking engine synced to MakeMyTrip, Goibibo, Booking.com and Agoda via your channel manager. The site goes live in 7 days.

Who the Heritage Theme Is Built For

Most website templates were designed for Western boutique hotels or generic service businesses. The Heritage theme was built from the ground up for the specific visual language, pricing context, and guest expectations of Indian heritage hospitality.

What the Heritage Theme Includes

Every element in this theme is chosen to amplify the atmosphere of an older property. Nothing generic, nothing corporate.

🏰

Full-Bleed Hero

Your best photograph fills the entire viewport. A warm gradient overlay and serif title create immediate atmosphere above the fold.

🔤

Serif Typography

Georgia-based serif font stack for headings. Warmer and more prestigious than the sans-serif fonts that most modern sites use.

🪟

Arch & Ornament Motifs

CSS-drawn arched window dividers and medallion ornaments between sections. No raster images — loads fast and scales cleanly.

🖼️

Story Gallery

Gallery layout with caption overlays that tell the property's history. Guests read while they browse photographs.

🛏️

Rooms & Suites Section

Each room type gets its own card with photos, description, occupancy, rate and direct booking or WhatsApp inquiry button.

🍽️

Dining & Experiences

Dedicated sections for rooftop dining, activities, cultural programmes and seasonal specials — the extras that justify your premium rate.

⭐

Guest Testimonials

Styled review showcase with guest names and travel context. Prominent placement builds trust before the guest reaches the booking step.

📱

Mobile-First Layout

Over 70% of Indian hotel searches happen on mobile. The Heritage theme looks and performs perfectly on Android and iPhone.

Rate parity insight: Your direct rate on your own website can include extras that OTA listings cannot — complimentary heritage walk, early check-in, or a welcome drink.
