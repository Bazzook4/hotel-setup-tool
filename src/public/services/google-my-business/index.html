  1. WhatsApp or call us

    Share your hotel name, location, and current GMB situation. We'll confirm the scope in 24 hours.

  2. Send us your hotel details

    Property name, address, contact number, website (if any), and 10–20 photos of rooms and property.

  3. We build or fix the profile

    We set up or correct your full Google Business Profile — category, hours, amenities, photos, booking link.

  4. Google verification

    We guide you through the Google verification step (phone, video, or postcard). Most complete in 1–3 days.

  5. Go live — listing transferred to you

    Once verified, the listing is transferred to your Google account. You own it completely.
