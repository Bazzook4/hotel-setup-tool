OTA vs Direct Booking — What's the Difference?

Factor OTA Booking (Airbnb / Booking.com) Direct Booking (Your Website)
Commission 15–18% per booking 0% — you keep everything
Guest data OTA owns it, you can't remarket You own it — build relationships
Pricing control Restricted by OTA policies Full control — offer direct discounts
Cancellation policy OTA sets the rules Your rules
Brand identity You're a listing, not a brand Your brand, your story
Payment speed 7–30 days payout delay Instant or at check-in
