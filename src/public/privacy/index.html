Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy and handle your data

Introduction

At OnlineHotelier Insights (insights.onlinehotelier.com), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website, tools, and guides.

By using our website, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide Directly

We do not require user registration to use our tools or access our guides. You can use all features of our site anonymously.

However, if you contact us via our contact form or email, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Message content

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

b) Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our site, we automatically collect certain information through cookies and analytics tools:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This includes pages viewed, time spent on site, device type, browser, and general location (city/country level).
  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve user experience and for analytics purposes.
  • IP Address: Your IP address is logged by our server and analytics tools but is not used to personally identify you.

c) Calculator and Tool Data

All calculator inputs (revenue, occupancy, rates, etc.) are processed entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any data you enter into our tools. Your data never leaves your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To improve our website: Analytics data helps us understand which guides and tools are most useful
  • To respond to inquiries: Contact form submissions are used only to answer your questions
  • To serve relevant ads: We use Google AdSense to display ads (see Section 4 below)
  • To ensure security: IP addresses and server logs help us prevent abuse and maintain site security

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or as described in this policy.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and understand how you interact with our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality
  • Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics): Track how visitors use our site to help us improve
  • Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense): Used to serve relevant ads

How to Control Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on our site. To learn more about cookies and how to manage them, visit aboutcookies.org.

4. Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track and analyze website traffic. Google Analytics collects information such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • General geographic location (city/country level)

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this data. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display ads on our website. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing activity across websites. You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Learn more about how Google uses data when you use our site: Google Partner Sites.

External Links

Our website may contain links to external websites (e.g., Booking.com, MakeMyTrip, OnlineHotelier.com). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Important: Since our calculator tools process all data in your browser, we never receive or store your sensitive business data (revenue, occupancy, etc.). Your information stays entirely on your device.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You can request information about the data we hold about you (if any)
  • Correction: You can request that we correct any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your information (e.g., contact form submissions)
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of cookies through your browser settings or opt out of personalized ads via Google Ads Settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: Contact Us

7. Children's Privacy

OnlineHotelier Insights is intended for use by hotel owners, managers, and hospitality professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
