About Hotel Playbook

Free tools and knowledge for Indian hotel owners

Our Mission

Hotel Playbook was created to help Indian hotel owners navigate the complexities of modern hospitality. From understanding OTA commission structures to implementing effective revenue management strategies, we provide the tools and knowledge you need to succeed.

We believe that every hotel owner, regardless of property size or budget, should have access to professional-grade revenue management tools and insights.

What We Offer

Free Tools - Our revenue management and OTA commission calculators are completely free. No signup, no hidden costs. Your data stays on your device.

Educational Guides - We're building a comprehensive library of guides covering OTA optimization, revenue management strategies, and hotel software tutorials - all tailored for the Indian market.

Practical Focus - Everything we create is designed to be actionable. No theory overload - just practical strategies you can implement immediately.
